HOW IS DIABETIC RETINOPATHY DETECTED?

There is no symptom of diabetic retinopathy in its early stages, but it is the most frightening yet preventable complication of diabetes. As the disease progresses, blood vessels in the retina bleed and eventually lead to the loss of sight if ignored.

ABOUT THE INITIATIVE

+

While the forces in uniform keep the nation protected with their watchful eyes, serving the people greatly, Netra Suraksha strives to protect the vision of them and their families. Through them we aim to encourage people to take eye disorders caused by diabetes seriously and go for regular eye exams. In its second phase, Network18's initiative Netra Suraksha - India Against Diabetes, in association with Novartis, continues to raise awareness about Diabetic Retinopathy through those who serve and protect the nation's citizens.
